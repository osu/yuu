Frequently Asked Questions

What is Yuu.sh ?

Yuu.sh is an temporary file uploader based on pomf.se

This service is free ?

Yes, but if you want to help us, you can make a donnation here :

Will you keep my files forever ?

No, we delete files every 24 hours to respect your privacy rights.

How can I keep track of my uploads ?

You can't, since we are aiming to provide a private and secure service we don't host any type of in-site accounts.

Why should I use this ?

We are aiming to provide a secure and fast service and also a big upload limit rate and we also delete all files after 24 hours (If the file is older than 24 hours it will be deleted).

What sort of information do you log on me ?

We don't log anything from you, we only keep your files that you uploaded and automatically delete them after 24 hours.

What are the allowed extensions here ?

We support any file extensions except the following: .nt, .ps1, .psm1, .bash_profile, .bashrc, .profile

How are the file URLs going to be determined ?

Random 5-letter strings will automatically be generated for your uploads.
